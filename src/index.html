How to play

Buy your 7Ticket, participate for a JackPot and support the project you like!




  • First step:

    The Lucky7 Numbers

    Each Monday, we are going to set "The Lucky7 Numbers".
    These are generated through our Pseudo-Random Number Generator, and the parameters to set them are going to be published on the Ethereum Blockchain.
    Both the Smart Contract and the PRNG are going to be published soon.

  • Second step:

    Buy your 7Ticket.

    You should buy two numbers to obtain a 7Ticket.
    With these and the PRNG we generate your 7ticket. Don't like your 7Ticket? You can buy new numbers.
    You can buy them individually or together.
    Once you are decided, you can buy your 7Ticket to participate in the lottery.
    You can even buy a 7Ticket directly if you feel lucky!

  • Third Step:

    Earn your 7Ticket and 7Token

    By buying a 7Ticket you win 2 things.
    First, you participate on the weekly concourse for the JackPot, where you can win prices in ETH depending on your luck and the amount of 7Tickets selled.
    Second, you earn a 7Token, our official token. With it, you can vote on any of the projects we are presenting in the "Our Projects" section.

  • Fourth Step:

    The JackPot

    If your 7Ticket is the closest to one of the Lucky7 Number amongst all the tickets, you become a Lucky7Ticket.
    There is going to be one Lucky7Ticket for every Lucky7 Number.
    Every Sunday we choose randomly three Lucky7Ticket to win 60%, 30% and 10% of the total pot.
    If your 7Ticket is exactly the same as a Lucky7 Number, you become a ExactLucky7Ticket and automatically win the biggest prize!.
    If there's more than one ExactLucky7Ticket, the prizes are going to be distributed on equal parts among all the ExactLucky7Ticket, leaving a 10% of the prize for the rest of the winners.
    The prizes depends on the amount of people buying tickets; the more people buys ticket, the greater the JackPot is going to be.

  • Fifth Step:

    Vote for your favorite project

    Choose a project you love and vote for it by giving your 7Tokens. Once every month, we will give a prize to the project with the most amount of 7okens.
    Vote responsibly and help the projects of the future.

  • Sixth Step:

    Fairplay Contract

    50% of the price of the 7Tickets are going to the Jackpot, 20% is going to the winner project and 30% is to keep Lucky7 working.
    The more people playing, the more chances we all have to win.
    Invite your friends, buy your tickets and help the world become a better place to live.

  • Buy a 7Ticket and try your luck!

About us

7Sit is a Start-Up commited to develop solutions which create benefits for every actor of our society.
We know that SITs (sustainable information technologies) can improve the way we live, but:
What is sustainability?
How can Information Technologies be sustainable?
It's all based on three pillars:


Social

Sustainability means benefits for everybody.
Our technologies give solutions which creates value to people and industries.

Ecologic

Recycle, save energy or decrease the carbon footprint.
Everything that can help our planet is good for us now and everyone in the future.

Economic

A sustainable business model needs a solid revenue stream.
Earning money is not the problem; the problem is the way it is generated.
The more money we earn, the faster we can achieve our goal to create a more sustainable society.

Our projects

Our biggest strengths today are Randomness and Blockchain.
Through Randomness we can achieve information security and through Blockchain we can create a trustless environment to create digital value.
Our projects are based on this two factors to create:

Mapa

Money transfer through Bitcoin

EnergIndex

IoT power consumption meter

Abroad Happiness

Sustainable parties for inmigrants all over the world.

Lucky7

Win-Win crowdfunding system.

Smart Plastic

Smart Contract based recycling economy

we are 7sit

Our goal is simple:
To spread and educate about the awesome benefits of sustainable business models, simple as that.
Our plan is based in 4 elements:




  • First Element:

    The Randomness Source

    We created a revolutionary Pseudo-Random Number Generator (PRNG) called M(b,n,mu).
    It can generate numbers of arbitrary length with reduced computational cost, based on 6 numeric parameters.
    With it we already developed information security technologies as a Symmetric Encryption Method (Mu Encryption) and Message Authetication Code.
    We are going to release it White Paper as soon as possible, but trust us, is pure randomness.

  • Second Element:

    The Trustless System

    Through Smart Contracts, people don't have to trust companies; all the business logic is stablished on software and is going to be executed according to it instructions.
    Everyone can take a look on the contract to verify that everything that is promised, is going to happen eventually.
    Through it we can create new tokens and rules to gobern communities to be sure everyone that participates in our projects wins something.

  • Third Element:

    The Founding System

    Through our PRNG and Smart Contracts, we created Lucky7.
    Lucky7 is a simple gambling game. You click the "Buy Ticket" button and you are going to be inmediatly participating for awesome prizes.
    Part of the ticket price goes to one of the three pots. The rest of it is going to be used to develop our projects. Everybody wins!

  • Fourth Element:

    Our Sustainable Projects

    Everyone of our projects designs are based on our sustainability's fundamental pillars.
    Look on the "About Us" and "Our projects" to know more.
    We know that the best way to educate and spread knowledge is through demonstrations.
    With our projects we don't just look for improving people lifes, but to show the society that the sustainability have direct and instant impact on everyone life, now and on the future.
    Support us and participate for a Jackpot!.
